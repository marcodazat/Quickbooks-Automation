📋 This policy governs the Daza Group internal bookkeeping automation tool ("Ledger AI") which integrates with QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API.

1. Introduction

Daza Group LLC ("Daza Group," "we," "us," or "our"), a Texas limited liability company, operates an internal bookkeeping automation tool ("the Application") that integrates with Intuit QuickBooks Online. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Application.

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 QuickBooks Financial Data

When you authorize the Application via Intuit OAuth 2.0, we access the following data from your QuickBooks Online account solely to provide bookkeeping services:

  • Chart of Accounts and account balances
  • Transaction records, invoices, and expenses
  • Company information and company name
  • Customer and vendor records
  • Profit & Loss and financial reports

2.2 API Credentials

The Application uses Intuit OAuth 2.0 access tokens to authenticate with QuickBooks Online. These tokens are stored only in your browser's session storage and are never transmitted to any third-party server operated by Daza Group.

2.3 User-Provided Information

We may collect information you directly provide, including bookkeeping rules, categorization preferences, and notes you enter into the Application.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we access exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide bookkeeping analysis and financial reporting for your QuickBooks Online account
  • To categorize transactions and suggest Chart of Accounts improvements
  • To parse remittance documents and match them to QuickBooks deposits
  • To generate journal entries and post them to QuickBooks on your behalf
  • To remember your bookkeeping preferences across sessions
We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose your financial data to any third party. Your QuickBooks data is used solely to deliver the services you request within the Application.

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

The Application operates primarily in your browser. OAuth tokens are stored in browser session storage and are cleared when the browser session ends. Bookkeeping rules and preferences are stored in browser local storage on your device only.

4.2 AI Processing

The Application uses the Anthropic Claude API to process your questions and financial data. Data sent to the Anthropic API is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy available at anthropic.com. We send only the minimum data necessary to answer your specific query.

4.3 No Central Database

Daza Group does not operate a central server or database that stores your QuickBooks financial data. All data processing occurs locally in your browser or transiently via the Intuit and Anthropic APIs.

5. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online API — for accessing your accounting data. Subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered analysis. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

6. Data Retention

Since the Application does not maintain a central database, financial data is not retained beyond your active browser session. Preferences stored in local storage remain on your device until you clear your browser data or disconnect from the Application.

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect the Application from your QuickBooks account at any time by revoking access through your Intuit account settings at accounts.intuit.com. Upon disconnection, no further data will be accessed.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application is intended solely for use by accounting professionals and business owners. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
